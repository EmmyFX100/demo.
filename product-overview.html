Safety and Compliance

Safety Warnings

Important Safety Instructions: Please read and follow these safety precautions before using your earbuds.

  • Avoid Exposure to High Volume: Listening at high volume for extended periods may result in permanent hearing damage. Use the lowest volume necessary.
  • Keep Away from Children: Small parts, including earbuds and tips, pose a choking hazard. Keep out of reach of children.
  • Use in Safe Environments: Do not use earbuds while operating machinery, riding bicycles, or in environments where hearing ambient sound is important.
  • Avoid Water Immersion: Although the earbuds may be water-resistant, do not submerge them or use them in showers or while swimming.
  • Charge Safely: Use only the included USB-C cable or certified chargers. Avoid overcharging or exposing the device to heat.
  • Do Not Disassemble: Opening the earbuds or charging case may void your warranty and pose a safety risk.
  • Avoid Extreme Temperatures: Do not expose the product to extremely high or low temperatures as it may affect battery life or cause malfunction.
  • Stop Use if Irritation Occurs: If you experience discomfort or irritation, discontinue use immediately and consult a medical professional if necessary.

Compliance Information

These Bluetooth Wireless Earbuds meet regulatory requirements and industry standards to ensure user safety and product quality.

FCC Statement (USA)

This device complies with part 15 of the FCC Rules. Operation is subject to the following two conditions:

  1. This device may not cause harmful interference, and
  2. This device must accept any interference received, including interference that may cause undesired operation.

CE Compliance (Europe)

This product complies with the essential requirements and other relevant provisions of the Radio Equipment Directive (2014/53/EU). A copy of the Declaration of Conformity is available upon request.

Environmental Compliance

This product is RoHS compliant and should be recycled according to WEEE Directive guidelines. Lithium-ion batteries should be recycled responsibly.
